Nat Pyper is an alphabet artist. They make fonts, write sci-fi, design wearables for videos and performances, and research queer publishing histories. Most recently, their work has been shown at Chuquimarca Projects, Gene Siskel Film Center, and Vox Populi. Their written work has been published by Are.na, Draw Down Books, GenderFail, Inga Books, Martian Press, Queer.Archive.Work, and the Walker Art Center. They are a 2018 graduate of the Yale School of Art.
